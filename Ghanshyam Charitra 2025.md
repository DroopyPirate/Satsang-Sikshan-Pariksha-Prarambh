

# 1 Ghanshyam no Janma

- Ayodhya paase chhapaiya. Gaam ni chaare baaju jaambu na vann chhe.
- Samvat 1837 Chaitra Sud Navmi (Monday, 3rd April 1781) raat na 10 vaage janma


# 2 Baad-Prabhu nu Parakram

- Kaalidatt rakshas. Krutyao ne mokli ghanshyam ne maarva.
- Krutyao ghar paase avi tyare, bhakti-mata ghanshyam ne dudh paata hata
- Krutyao ghanshyam ne upadi ne aamba na vann taraf bhaagi.
- Aamba na vann ma pavan-putra hanumanJi rahe.
- Krutyao ne bau maari (chotla puchdi ma bandhina pachadi). Krutyao e kidhu "Ane pacha Chhapaiya nai aaiye, amne java do".
- Pachi hanumanJi ghanshyam prabhu ne laine ghare bhakti-mata ne api ne adrashya thai gaya.
- Krutyao e kaalidatt ne vaat kari. Kaalidatt e sauthi moti krutya, Kotra ne mokli.
- Kotra ghare pase aai ane ghanshyam ekhla pade eni raah joti hati.
- Ghanshyam e ek vaanki najar kari ane kotra badva laagi ane cheeso paadti paadti tyaj mari gai.
- Badha dari gaya ane navai paami gaya. Ghanshyam ne andarna orda ma lai jai ne suvdai didha


# 3 Raamdayad ne Darshan

- Ghanshyam prabhu 2.5 months na hata.
- Bhaktimata utavad ma prabhu ne parna ma suvdavi ne rasoda ma jata rahya.
- Prabhu parna mathi utari ne, dur padeli chusni haath ma lai pacha parna ma besi gaya.
- Raamdayad je dharmadev na mitra hata emne aa joyu ane ashcharya thi puchyu ke "Tamara putra ni ummar ketli che"?
- Ummar jaani ne emne thayu ke kharekhar aato ishwar j hova joiye, nani ummar e aatli chaturai.
- Aam vichari prabhu na parna pase gaya to prabhu na sharir mathi tej na kirno nikadva laagya.


# 4 Prabhu nu Naam paadyu

- Prabhu 3 month na thai gaya.
- Maarkanday muni dharmadev na ghare avya.
- Emne aasan apyu ane emni puja kari.
- Dharmdev e kahyu "Tame prakhyat ane vidvaan jyotishi chho, mara putra nu naam paado ane bhavishya bhaakho".
- Muni e tipnu kholi, aangdi na vedha ganva  laagya:
	- Kark raashi ma janm - etle Hari
	- Sharir no rang shyam - etle Krushna
	- Bhegu kariye - etle HariKrushna
	- Ghanshyam kahi ne pan bolavse
	- Putra ma tap, tyag, yog, dharm, niti, satya, vivek badha guno chhe - etle Nilkanth naam thi jaanita thase
	- Pruthvi par dharm nu sthapan karse
	- Tamara ane loko na dukho no naash karse
	- Desh ma charo kor emni kirti felase
	- Loko ne samadhi karavse
	- Loko no udhar karse
	- Bhagvan nu bhajan karavi, saune sukhi karse
- Dharmdev raaji thai, muni ne sara abhushan-vastro, gaayo ane sona-mahor dakshina ma aapya


# 5 Putra ni Pariksha

- Baajot upar resham nu vastra patharyu.
- Baajot upar sona-mahor, pustak, ane nani talvaar muki.
- Parna mathi utri ne, baajot pase avi ne pustak upadi lidhu.
- Mata pita ne khatri thai ke chokkas a putra bhani gani ne vidvaan thase.


# 6 Ghanshyam e kaan vindhavya

- 7 mahina na ghanshyam.
- Bhaktimata ghanshyam ne lai ne ambali na jhaad ni saame osari par betha.
- Kaan chindhva vada ghanshyam mathi tej nikadta joyu, badhe tej dekhai, tene chees padi.
- Pachi tej pachu lai lidhu ane ghanshyam mata na khoda mathi adrshya thai gaya.
- Ghansyam aambli ni daad par dekhaya. Mata e RaamPratap bhai ne bolavya, ghanshyam ne niche utarva.
- RaamPratap bhai upar chadya to ghanshyam niche mata na khoda ma dekhaya. Niche utrya to banne jagya e dekhai.
- Pachi biju swaroop ghanshyam e adrashya kari kahyu "Mane gaud khava apo to kaan vindhavish."
- "Jo dahya thai ne kaan vindhava bessho to gaud khava madse." Pachi ghanshyam e khoda ma betha betha gaud khata kaan vindhavyo.


# 7 Siddhio Prabhu ni Seva ma

- Prabhu na maami - Lakshmibaai.
- Bhakti mata e maami ne kahyu hatu ke haji vaar chhe etle shanti thi sheero banavjo.
- Maami shanti thi sheero banavta hata pan bhakti mata ne bhukh laagi.
- Prabhu e jaani ne mano mann 8 siddhio ne bhojan laavva aagna kari.
- 8 siddhio 32 pakwaan lai ne avi gai ne bhakti mata ne kahyu "'Prabhu e amne aagna kari chhe ke tamne bhukh laagi chhe etle bhojan laine avo."
- Mata achkata hata etle, prabhu avya ane pote bhojan lidhu ane maata ne kahyu ke "Tame pan jamo."
- Maami ne aa joine navai lagi. Siddhio kahe "Mataji ame tamara mate roj bhojan laavsu."
- Ghanshyam e kahyu ke "Roj na laavta, khali jyare maami bhojan banava ma vaar lagade tyare lavjo."


# 8 Khichdi ni badle DudhPaak

- Ghanshyam bhukhya hata. Maata e taadhi kichdi khava api ane kahyu "Tame a khavanu sharu karo hu dudh laine avu."
- Gomti naam ni gaay hati. Savar saanj be vaar dudh ape.
- Bhakti mata gomti pase gaya pan dudh nikde nahi. Ghanshyam tya avya. Mata kahe "Haji dudh ne ghani vaar chhe."
- Gomti ne ghanshyam par vhaal ane ghanshyam ne joie tyare dudh ape.
- Ghanshyam e pavalu dharyu, e bharai gayu. Maata andar thi doni lai avya e pan bharai gayu. Etle maata farithi moti doni lai avya, ane e pan bharava lagi.
- Mata ne thayu ke "Ghar na badha vasano bharai jase." Ghanshyam kahe "Mari ichha thi dudh ni dhaar nikadti hati, have bandh thai jase."
- Pachi dudh bandh thyu. Bhakti mata ne navai laagi. Mata e prasanna thai ne kahyu "Aatlu badhu dudh chhe to tame tadhi khichdi na khaso, hu dudhpaak-puri banavi dau."


# 9 Vaaland ne Chamatkaar

- Ghanshyam 3 varas na thaya. Dharm pita ne thayu ke have vaad utraavi daiye.
- Bija divse saaru murat joine Amai naam na Vaaland ne bolavyo.
- Bhakti maata ghanshyam ne lai ne Purva (East) disha ma betha ane Amai Vaaland e adhi hajaamat puri kari.
- Adhi hajaamat e Vaaland ne ghanshyam dekhavana bandh thai gaya. Badha ne dekhai pan vaaland ne na dekhai.
- Bhakti maata kahe "Ubha kem rahi gaya, hajaamat chalu rakho". Vaaland kahe ke mane Ghanshaym dekhataj nathi.
- Mata e ghanshyam ne kaan ma kahyu "Adhi hajaamat karelu modhu saru na laage. Maate hajaamat puri karai lo, Amai ne darshan apo."
- Pachi Amai saame drashti kari etle Amai ne ghanshyam na darshan thaya.
- Hajaamat pachi ghanshyam e Narayan Sarovar ma snaan lidhu.
- Pita e bhrahmano ne daan apyu, Patasaa apya ane bhrahmano ne jamaadvani taiyari ma laagi gaya.


# 10 Kaalidatt maran ne sharan

- Maat pita kaam ma hata.
- Badha baad-mitro ramva maate Narayan sarovar na purva (East) ma Ambavaadi ma gaya. Tya aambli-pipdi ramva laagya.
- Tya Kaalidatt avyo ane Ghanshyam ne pakadva hath laambo karyo. Ghanshyam e teni saame drashti kari ane te badva laagyo.
- Kaalidatt ghusse thayo ane teni jadui shakti thi tene bhayankar vaavajhodu ane varsaad sharu karya. Badhe andharu.
- Bau thandi ane varsaad thi, baadako dhrujva laagya ane mota Vadd na jhaad na bakhol ma santai gaya.
- Ghanshaym aamba na jhad ni niche jaine betha.
- Kaalidatt ne ghashyam aamba na jhaad par besela dekhaya. Tene potanu sharir akaash sudhi uchu karyu.
- Ane jor thi aamba jhaad par kudyo. Jhaad tuti gayu ane Ghanshyam jhaad na niche kachdai gaya.
- Kaalidatt e badhi daadkhi kaadhi ne joyu to Ghanshyam em ne em betha hata.
- Kaalidatt e ghussa ma hath lambavyo. Ghanshyam e kardi najar kari etla ma Kaalidatt, jhaado sathe athdata athdata mari gayo.
- Maata pita dodta dodta aamba na vaann ma avya. Ghanshyam madya etle badha anand ma avi gaya.


# 11 Taadhe paani e badiya gaya

- Grishma rutu (Unado, summer). Ghanshyam ne taav etle jamya nahi.
- Chanda maasi khabar kadhva avya ane hath feryo to khabar padi ke badiya nikdya chhe.
- Maata e ghanshyam ne suvdavi ne bhagwan nu bhajan karva lagya.
- Maami lakshmibaai e kahyu ke 20 divas sudhi baar na java deta ane navdavta pan nai.
- Ghanshyam kahe ke apde to brahman aapde nahya vagar na  chale. Maata ne kahe ke thandu paani lavo, hu naaish etle taav ane badiya mati jase.
- Ane maata e thandu paani sichi apyu, ane badhu mati gayu, khali zhakha daagha rai gaya.
- Lakshmibaai ane chanda maasi ne thayu aa nakki bhagwan chhe. Pranam karine ghare gaya.


# 12 Maachalio sajivan kari

- Chhapaiya ma ghanshyam ek vaar, Veni, Maadhav ane Praagna ne laine Meen Sarovar nahva gaya.
- Sarovar kinare ek vadd nu vruksh. Tya kapda kadhine badha nahva taiyaar thaya.
- Ghanshyam ni najar maachimaar par padi. Kado ane kadavar maachimaar.
- Sankalp karyo ane badhi maachli jivit thai ne sarovar ma kudva laagi.
- Maachimaar ghusse thai maarva dodyo.
- Ghanshyam e enu abhimaan todva, yamraaj nu roop lidhu.
- Yamraaj nu roop bhayaanak, laambaa daat ane laambi jeebh, 18  hath ane darek hath ma hathiyaar.
- Yamraaje yampuri ane narak na dukho dekhadya. Yam duto paase maar khavdavi haadka khokhra kari nakhya.
- Maachimaar bumo paadva lagyo, tene bhul samjai ane kidhu ke havethi hu nai karu, dhandho badli nakhis. Tame bhagwan chho mara paapo dur karo.
- Ghanshyam e tene ashirwaad api ravana karyo.


# 13 Chaklaao ne samaadhi

- Chhapaiya baaju Targaam na padare Dharmdev nu khetar hatu. Daangar paaki ne taiyaar ane chaklaa bagadi ane emne aaje bije gaam javanu hatu etle ghanshyam ne khetar sambharva kidhu.
- Maata  e ghanshyam ne jamaadya, maathe topi pehravi, makhmal ni mojdi pervai ane ek soti api ne khetare moklya.
- Khetare ghanshyam e joyu ke bau badha chakla chhe. Ek saad padyo ema badhane samaadhi.
- Pachi baaju nu khetar MaadhavRaam Shukla nu tya potana mitro Raghuveer ane BakusRaam jode ramva laagya.
- Saanje Dharmdev ane RaamPratap bhai avya ne puchyu ke khetar nu dhyan rakhva kidhu hatu ne.
- Ghanshyam kahe to chalo khetare. Tya joyu to vichar ayo ke aatla badha chakla mari gaya?
- Ghanshyam e fari saad apyo ema to badha chakla udi gaya.
- Dharmdev ane RaamPratap ne thayu ke nakki aa Sarvopari Bhagwan chhe ane badha na naadi praan amna hath ma chhe.


# 14 Vaandra ne Samaadhi

- Chhapaiya ma asuro nu traas vadhi gayo hato. Etle pita e Ayodhya javano vichar karyo.
- Shangaarela gaada ma ghar nu badhu Raacharchilu muki ne sah-kutumb nikdya.
- Gaadu saryu nadi ne kinare ayu tyare gharni badhi vastu, hodi ma muki ne saryu ne bije kaanthe ayodhya pochya.
- Ayodhya ma Barhatta sheri ma navu ghar ma vasvaat karyo.
- Saanje pita na baju ma ghanshyam jamva betha.
- Jhaad par thi ek vandro avi ne 20 rotli lai gyo ane jhaad par besi gayo.
- Batku bhare e pela ghanshyam e drashti kari ne vaandra ne samaadhi thai gai.
- 3 divas sudhi samaadhi ma rahyo vaandro.
- Pachi jagyo ane ghar ma avi ne ghanshyam ne hath jodi, pag paase besi gayo.
- Ghanshyam e ashirwaad apya ane thodu khavanu apyu.


# 15 Vaandrao ne methipaak chakhadyo

- Ghanshyam puri ane dahi laine osari ma jamva aya.
- Ek vandro ayo, hath mathi puri khechi ne aambali na jhaad par jai besi gayo.
- Ghanshyam e hath laambo karyo jhaad sudhi ane gardan pakdi ne vaandra ne pachhadyo.
- Vaandro chichiyari paadva lagyo etle bau vandra bhega thai gaya ane ghanshyam ne gheri lidhaa.
- Dharmdev ne joine thayu ke aa vandrao ghanshyam ne maari nakhse, etle laakdi leva andar gaya.
- Ghanshyam e jetla vaandra hata etla potana swaroop kadhya ane vandaro ne methipaak alyo.
- Vandrao bhaagi gaya ane dharmdev e aai ne joyu to ascharya lagyu ane thayu ke nakki aa bhagwaan j chhe.


# 16 Raamdatt ne keri chakhadi

- Ghanshyam mitro sathe sarovar na nahva gaya.
- Khub ramya, nahya pachi bhukh laagi.
- Aambavadi ma gaya. Ghanshyam uppar thi keri naakhe. Veni, Maadhav ane Pragna keri bhegi kare ane bija badha aamba ni chauki kare.
- Bhramano no sang tyathi nikdyo. Sang no aagevaan Raamdatt. sharire tagdo.
- Ramdatt bhramano ne kahe aa badhi keri lai lo.
- Upar thi ghanshyam e joyu. Raamdatt keri leva niche vadyo tya ghanshyam e dadkhi par thi Raamdatt na khabha par thi dori ane loto lai lidho.
- Raamdatt ghusse thai ne jhaad par chadhyo. Ghanshyam dadkhi badle rakhe.
- Raamdatt e bhramano ne pan jhaad par chadhva kidhu. Ghanshyam sauthi upar ni dadhki par jato rahyo.
- Raamdatt upar java gayo, Ghanshyam adrashya thai gaya ane jhaad na niche dekhaya.
- Ghanshyam mitro ne kahe ke bhramano ne niche na utarva deta. Kaankra maro.
- Badha bhramano e chhelle maafi maangi ane potana raste gaya.


# 17 Mumukshu kai disha ma che?

- Chhapaiya gaam ni bahar peepda nu jhaad.
- Ghanshyam ghani vaar ramvanu chhodi ne peepda na jhaad par chadi jaai ane paschim ma joya kare.
- Ek vaar evuj thayu, ghanshyam niche utrya tyare Veni e puchyu ke paschim disha ma kem joya karo cho?
- "Hu mumukshu kai disha ma chhe e jou chhu. Aiya thi hajaro gaam dur Paschim ma gujarat ma kathiyavaad desh chhe, tya shri krushna ni dwarka chhe. Tya bhagwan ne malva talsi rahya mumukshu rahe chhe. Mare ek divas ahi thi tya javu chhe. Himalay ane bharat na tirtho jova chhe pan rehvu to kathiyawaad maj chhe."


# 18 Ek sathe anek Mandire darshan

- Ghanshyam bapore jamva avya nahi. Maata e RaamPratap bhai ne ghanshyam ne laavva moklya.
- RaamPratap bhai sidha Hanuman gadhi gaya. Tya ghanshyam RaamKatha saambhdta hata. Amna aavu em kahi ne bhai ne moklya.
- Rasta ma bau mandiro ave etle RaamPratap bhai darshan karva ek mandire gaya. Tya pan ghanshyam RaamKatha sambhadta hata.
- Etle navai laagi etle fari Hanuman gadhi gaya to tya to bethaj hata.
- Kutuhalta na karane bija mandiro ma pan joyu to tya pan ghanshyam dekhaya.
- Ghare avi ne mata pita ne vaat kari. Thodi vaar ma ghanshyam avya ane mata pita sathe besi ne bhojan karyu.


# 19 Bhutiyo Kuvo

- Ayodhya ma ek navab ayo hato. E badhane traas apto, loko ne lute, badako ne upadi jaai.
- Ena traas thi bachva Dharmdev sah parivaar, Teenva gaam ma Prathit Paande ane emna patni Vachnabaai na ghare rokaya.
- Bhaktimata ne Vachnabaai e kidhelu ke Suryaast pachi kuva ma paani bharva na jata tya 1000 o bhuto rahe chhe.
- Ek vaar dhyan na rahyu ane gaya. Ghado nakhyo, paani ma pade e pehla bhuto e pakdi lidho, nichhe joyu to 1000 o bhuto.
- Dodta ghare aya ane badhi vaat kari.
- Ghanshyam bija divse savare kuva ma padya, gaam vada naa padta hata to pan.
- Badha bhuto jaagi gaya ane, ane ghashyam ne pakadva gaya.
- Ghanshyam mathi tej nikdyu ane badha badva lagya.
- Badha bhuto e maafi mangi ane ajiji kare ke hey prabhu bachavo.
- Ghanshyam e emnu kuva ma revanu karan puchyu.
- Ghana varsho pela a badha loko no baadshah sathe jhagdo thayelo ane aa badha paapi, vyasani, maas khata ane baadshah ni sathe ladai ma marya pachi paap ne lidhe bhut banya.
- Ghanshyam e emno uddhar karyo ane emne Badrikashram moklya.
- E pachi kuva ma koi bhut bachya nai ane gramya jano ne shanti thai.


# 20 Mallo no Parajay

- Roj saanje ayodhya ma RaamGhaat par Ghanshyam Kesari sang ane mitro sathe kusti daav karva jata.
- Ek vaar savare karta hata ane Nepal no Mahabali pehelvaan emne joine hasva laagyo. Strongest pehelvaan hato.
- Ene Ghanshyam e haravyo, teni vaat  akha Ayodhya ane aju baju vaada gaam ma felai gai.
- Ayodhya na mall BheemSang, DilliSang ane MaanSang  e aa vaat jaani ne Ayodhya na Raja RaaiDarshanSinh ne kahyu ke ame Ghanshyam ne haraiye.
- Raja RaaiDarshanSinh e emne chetvya ke eto bhagwan chhe, emni agad naa jiti saakai.
- Mallo e kidhu ke tame pan jojo ane ame jitye to amne bakshish apjo.
- Raja e akha gaam ma dhindhoro pitavyo ke "Aaje saanje Barhatta sheri ma Dharmdev na ghar pase Aambli vada kuva pasena medan ma aa tran mallo ane ghanshaym nu mall yudh thase. Badha e haajar rehvu."
- Medan saaf thai gayu, maandvo nakhi didho, bethak gothvi didhi, dhaja-pataka lehrai didhi.
- Dharmdev ne chinta thai ke aa malo ghanshyam ne maari na de.
- Ghanshyam e emne motu mall nu swaroop dekhadyu ane kahyu ke Bhagwan potani laaj rakhse. Pachi swaroop nanau kar didhu, Dharmdev ne shanti thai
- Sauthi moto mall BheemSang e ghanshyam ne medaaan na vache bolai ne potana pag saakad baandhi ne ubho rahyo.
- Ene kidhu ke mara jamna pag ma 100 haathi jetlu bad chhe. Maro jamno pag halave ke khasade to hu maanu.
- Ghanshyam e daabe hathe saakad uchi kari, BheemSang ucchdi ne aambli na jhaad e thokayo, saakad tuti gai. Modha ane naak mathi loi nikadva laagyu.
- Ghusse ayo etle saakad laine ayo ane kidhu ke saakad pag ma badhine ubho reh pachi ame tane fudardi feraiye.
- Ghanshyam ubha rahya. Trane mallo o bau jor lagayu pan ghanshyam halej nahi. Saakad tuti gai ane trane mallo padi gya. Haadka tuti gaya ane ubhu pan na thavai.
- Raja e Ghanshyam ne jai bolvani, mallo ne maafi mangva kidhu. Mallo kahe aaj fari taakat nu abhimaan nai kariye.
- Raja e dharmdev ane ghanshyam ne hathi par besadi ne vaajte-gaajte feravya.


# 21 Kandoi ne Parcho

- Suvaasini Bhaabhi rasoi banavta hata. Ghanshyam avi ne kahe "bhukh laagi chhe."
- Bhaabhi e kahyu ke haji thodik vaar chhe tya sudhi Chana ke Sukhdi apu.
- Ghanshyam kahe ke emne penda j khava chhe. Bhaabhi ne navai laagi ke koi divas bhukh laagi hoi to keh nai ane ema pan mishtaan ma to jarai ruchi nathi.
- Lot baandhta bhaabhi e vinti baaju ma muki ane ghanshyam ne kahyu ke penda to Kandoi-mithaadvada pase madse aiya nai.
- Ghanshyam dodta dodta vinti laine bhagya. Bhaabhi ghanshyam ne pakadva bhagya pan hath ma na ave. Ek ordaa thi bija thi trija ma. Bhaabhi e emne ordaa ma bandh kari didha.
- Bhaabhi e fari ne joyu to ghanshyam chauk ma ubha hata ane tya thi naasi gaya.
- Kandoi paase jaine kidhu ke dukaan ni badhi mithai aap hu tane aa vinti aapis. Kandoi e lalach ma badhi mithaai api didhi.
- Ghanshyam mitro ne lai ne vaadi e jaine ujaani kari.
- Thodi vaar ma DharmPita ane RaamPratap bhai avya. Bhabhi e badhi vaat kari.
- Etla ma ghanshyam dodta aaine Maata ni god ma besi gaya.
- RaamPratap bhai e puchyu e vinti kya gai Ghanshyam? Ghanshyam kahe me mane vinti vishe nathi khabar etle emne ek chamaat padi.
- Ghanshyam rova laagya etle bhabhi e emne tedi lidha ane maata pan vacche padya.
- Chhevate pita, bhai ane ghanshyam kandoi ni dukane gaya. RaamPratap bhai e puchyu ke aa Ghanshyam vinti api gayo chhe?
- Kandoi e kahyu ke aa vinti ne badle me ghanshyam ne dukaan ni badhi mithaai api didhi chhe, andar badhu khali chhe.
- Ghanshyam e radta radta kahyu ke "Na na, aa khotu bole chhe, me teni mithai nathi lidhi. Chalo teni dukan ma".
- Dukan ma andar gaya to badha topla mithai thi bharela hata. Kandoi ne navai laagi ane tene hath jodi ne kahyu ke "Tamaro putra mahaan chhe."
- Pachi ashcharya thi badha vinti laine pacha ghare avya.


# 22 Khaapa Talavdi

- Ek vaar ghanshyam RaamPratap bhai ane Suvaasini Bhaabhi sathe Targaam gaya hata.
- Ghar ni baaju ma ek nanakdu vann, tema ek talavdi hati. Talavdi ne chharo kor ghaas etle govada gaayo charava tya avta. Ghanshyam talavdi e Aambli-Pipdi ramva avta.
- Ek sanje govado pachha jata hata tyare Ghanshyam e saad apyo etle badhi gaayo, ghanshyam ne arte farte ubhi rahi gai. Govada kahe pan gaay hale nai. Ghanshyam e badhi gayo par hath muki ne pachi kidhu ke have Jao, tyare gaayo gai.
- Pachi Ghanshyam Aambli na vruksh par chadi ne thodi vaar ramya. Niche utarta pag lapsyo ane niche padya tya Jamna pag na sathad ma khaapo vagyo.
- Sukhnandan RaamPratap bhai ne bolava gayo. Etla ma akash mathi Devo avya ane temne AshwiniKumar ne bolavya. AhwiniKumar e aayi ne pag ma dava lagai ane paato bandhyo. Pachi devo Ghanshyam ne page laagi ne adrashya thai gaya.
- RaamPratap bhai e avi ne puchyu ke aa paato kone bandhyo. Tyare Veni e kahyu ke AshwiniKumare.
- RaamPratap bhai raaji thai ne Ghanshyam ne tedi ne ghare lai gaya. Suvaasini Bhabhi e ghanshyam ne chhati e chapya ane puchyu kya vagyu? Ghanshyam e kahyu ke mane vagyuj nathi. Paato kholi ne joyo to khali Khaapo ghaa dekhayo biju kai dekhayu nai.


# 23 Sod Chinho

- **Dharmadev, BhaktiMaata, Tran Putro, VasantaaBai ane ChandaaMaasi** sathe tirth yaatra e nikdya.
- Rasta ma **Gunda gaam** avyu. Saanje gaam na mandir ma aarti karva gaya.
- Chalu aarti e Thakorji ni murti aasan thi niche utri ne gada mathi haar utari ne ghanshyam ne pehravyo.
- Badha e joyu. Vaat felayi. Aa vaat **Raja GumaanSinh** ne khabar padi.
- Raja e hukam moklavyo ke kale **savare 10 vage** DharmDev ane temna 3 putro darbar ma hajar thai.
- Savare Raja e DharmDev ne puchyu ke ama thi Ghanshyam kon chhe. Dharmdev e vachla putra Ghanshyam ne bataya,
- Raja ne thayu ke jo aa badak Bhagwan hoi to shashtra pramane eno padchhayo na hoi ane pag ma 16 chinho hova joiye.
- Raja e badhane choganma bolaya. Joyu to badhano padchhayo dekhai pan Ghanshyam no na dekhai.
- Pachi Raja e Ghanshyam ne pag laamba kari ne besva kahyu.
- Pag ma joyu to **Jamna pag ma - AshtKon, UrdhvaRekha, Swastik, Jambu, Jav, Vajra, Ankush, Ketu ane Padma - em 9 chinho** joya.
- **Daba pag ma Trikon, Kadash, Gopad, Dhanush, Minn, ArdhChandra ne Vyom em 7 chinho.**
- **Banne pag ma UrdhvaRekha Angutha ane Aangdi vachethi nikdi ne paani taraf jaai**.
- Raja anand ma aai gaya ane pachi Raja ane  **Raani KuvarBaa** page lagya, pooja kari, aarti  kari, **makhmal ni sonani bharatvaadi topi ane survaad bhet apya.**
- Trije divse Gunda Gaam thi nikdya. IcchaRaam nana hata, chalta avde nai etle **Vasantabaai IchhaRaam ne tedi ne chhale**. Ane Ghanshyam BhaktiMata ne angdi pakdi ne chale.
- Ghanshyam e hath kari BhaktiMata ne ke tame  Ichhraam ne tedo chho pan mane nai, IchhaRaam no bhaar vadhare chhe, maro bhaar chej nai.
- **Ghanshyam e IchhaRaam no bhar vadhari didho**. VasantaBaai Bhaktimata ne kahe ke  IchhaRaam aatla bhare kem lage chhe.
- BhaktiMaata e kahyu ke tame Ghanshyam ne tedo. Emno bhaar na lagyo. Pachi IchhRaam ne upadya to emno pan bhaar na lagyo.
- Yatraa karta karta **Lucknow ane Kaanpur thai ne Ayodhya** taraf pacha farya.


# 24 Ghanshyam e hinsaa bandh karavi

- **Dharmdev na saadu bhai BaldiDhar. BaldiDhar ane emna bhai MorliGangaDhar banne Raja na sipahi**
- Raja nu lashkar **BallamPadhri gaam** avyu. Dharmdev ne thayu ke naji chhe etle BaldiDhar ane MorliGangaDhar ne mali aviye.
- Gaam ne paadre bagicha ma lashkar hatu. Tya darwaje ek chaukidaar hato. Dharmdev e kahyu ke mara aa banne ne malyu chhe. **Chaukidaar Pipda na jhaad paase lai gayo**. Tya tambu hato.
- Tambu ma Raaja sipahio paase bakra, gaay vagere ni hinsa karavto hato.
- Hinsa joi Dharmdev dukhi thaya e joine Ghanshyam e puchyu ke su che?
- Ghanshyam e andar joine sankalp karyo etle badha hathi ane ghoda potani saakad todine tambu taraf dodya ane tambu ukhadva laagya. Sipahio bhagva lagya. Hathi ghoda emni pachad bhaagya.
- Raja tambu ma ughade sharire betho hato e pan bhagyo. Eni pachad hathi bhagyo. Raja ni dhotli chhuti gai. Jem tem dhotli pakdi ne pipda na jhaad niche santayo. Hathi e taraf j ayo etle raja upar chadi gayo ane hathi agad nikdi gayo.
- Ghanshyam e jhaad ma pravesh karine, jhaad halava lagyo. Raja gabhrayo ane maafi mangva lagyo.
- Ghanshyam e jhaad rahine bolya ke hinsa kare chhe paap kare chhe. Ane je bhagwan ne tu bolave chhe e to niche Dharmdev ni bajuma ubha chhe.
- Raja e bau maafi mangi pachi Ghanshyam e jhaad sthir karyu. Raja e niche aaine maafi mangi ne kahyu ke aaj thi hu ane maru senya koine hinsa nai karva dau. Mara paap maaf karo.
- Ghanshyam e raja ne ashirwad apya ane pachi e visaamo leva tambu ma gayo. Ane dharmdev, RaamPratap ane Ghanshyam, BaldiDhar ane MorliGangaDhar ne mali ne Ayodhya gaya.


# 25 Mahaavat ni Rakshaa kari

- **Ayodhya ma Baldevji naam na sheth** hata emne hathi paadyo ane ene sambhadva mahaavat rakhyo.
- Mahaavat roj hathi na khorak mathi **ghee ni mithaio chori le**.
- Hathi badhu juve. Ek vaar sarovar ma snan karava hathi ne mahaavat lai gayo.
- Hathi e ene pachhadva maate sundh thi pakdine ucho karyo.
- Dharmdev ane Ghanshyam tyathi jata hata emne aa joyu.
- Ghnashyam e biju swaroop pragat kari ne sarovar ma utaryu.
- Mahaavat ne chhodavyo. Hathi par ghanshyam besi ne sarovar ni baar laya, shant karyo.
- Biju swaroop adrashya karyu. Hathi par besi ne potana na aangna sudhi avya.
- Mahaavat e hathi ne besva kidhu, haathi betho, badha utrya.
- Mahaavat e ghanshyam ne aabhar ane maafi mangi ane kahyu ke havethi khorak ni chori nai karu.

# 26 Navi Batreesi

- Ghanshyam to Suvaasini Bhaabhi "Aaje dadh dukhe chhe to jamvama **sheero aapjo mane**."
- Bhaaabhi e badha ne rotli pirsi ane Ghanshyam ne sheero apyo. Ghanshyam e emathi **thodo sheero IchhaRaam ne apyo** ane **2 kodiyo sheero khaine** ubha thai gaya.
- Pachi **rasoda pachad na fadiya ma** Ghanshyam ne Bhaabhi e puchyu ke daadh bau dukhe chhe?
- Ghanshyam e ek ek karine aa daat dukhe chhe em kari ne badha daat bhaabhi pase padavi didha. (**potani shakti thi daat dhila kari** didhela)
- Bhaabhi gabhrai gya ane Mata ne bolaya. Maata e daat ni dhagli joi ane Ghanshyam ne modhu kholva kidhu.
- Joyu to Ghanshyam ne **navi Batreesi avi gayi** hati.
- Ghanshyam e daat ni dhagli mutthi ma bhegi kari ane hath uchho karyo, to badha **daat moti ma badlai gaya**. 
- Etla ma akaash mathi **MaanSarovar na RaajHanso avya** ne udi udi ne ek ek moti lai ne udya.


# 27 BaadMitro ne jamaadya

- **Chhapaiya - Meen Sarovar. Maadhav, Veni, Praag, Sukhnandan jode ramta ramta 4 vagya**.
- Badha e vicharyu jaldi ghare jaine jamiye.
- Ghanshyam kahe mari jode **saanj sudhi ramo** to aiyaj jamvani vyavastha karu.
- Badha maanya. Ghanshyam e **aamba ni daadi par rumaal bandhyo** ane kahyu ke thodi vaar ma mithai avse. Tya sudhi apde sarovar ma snaan kariye.
- Paani ma khub ramat kari. Ghanshyam e badhane baar kadhya ke modu thai jase pachi motabhaai bolse.
- VeniRaam e kidhu ke Ghanshyam mithaio jamadvvanu kahyu tu.
- Ghanshyam e daadkhi par no rumaal kholi ne mithaio kadhva lagya. Badhane thayu rumaal ma thi kemnu.
- Ghanshyam jaani gaya. Ghanshyam e kidhu ke badha besi jao ane Ghanshyam e sankalp karyo etle aakash mathi **8 siddhio utari ne avi ane emani paase 32 pakwaan** ane **sonani jhaari ma thandu paani**.
- Chhelle siddhio prabhu ne page laagi ne adrashya thai ane badha pacha ghare gaya.


# 28 Hajaaro ne jamaadya ane Abhimaan todyu.

- **Sanyasio ni jamaat Chapaiyaa ayi. 1000 sadhu bavao. Badha Khaapa Talavdi ni kinare rokaya**.
- **Nahi Dhoine 5 aagevaan sanyasio gaam ma avya**, sidhu saaman leva.
- Aatla badha nu sidhu mangse ena bik thi **gaam no dhani MotiBhai Tarvaadi bije gaam jata rya**.
- 5 sadhuo gaam ma sauthi mota sajjan kon chhe em puchine Dharmdev na ghare avya.
- Dharmdev e kahyu ke **100 jetla nu sidhu apu**, etluj chhe, biju gaam ma thi bhegu kari lejo.
- Bavao e hath pakdi maane nai. Ghanshyam ghare aai ne pitane puchuy ke su thayu.
- Vaat janya pachi kidhu ke sidhu saaman kadho khutse nai. **Suvaasini bhabhi badhu kadhva lagya**, jot jotama **1000 manas thi vadhare nu sidhu no dhaglo thai gayo**.
- Bavao khush thai ne gaya. Dharmdev ne thayu ke have ghar ma kai bachyu nai hoi.
- Em vichari ne **kothar ma joyu pan kothar ma badhu emnu em hatu**.


# 29 LakshmiBaai ne Chamatkaar.

- Ghani vaar Ghanshyam mitro sathe adosi padosi na ghare jai makham, dai khai ave.
- **VeniRaam jode ena ghare jaine dahi** ma saakar nakhi khava lagya. **VeniRaam na maata LaxmiBaai je baar dana vinta hata**.
- Andar aaine joyu, banne jana **baari ma thi naasi gaya**.
- LaxmiBaai e BhaktiMaata ne fariyaad kari. BhaktiMaata kahe ke Ghanshyam evi chori karej nai. VeniRaam ghani vaar amare ghare aaine khai jaai chhe. **Ghanshyam evu karto hoi to ene pakdi rakhjo to maanva ma ave**.
- **Thoda divas pachi** pacha VeniRaam ane Ghanshyam banne Dahi khava gaya. **LaxmiBaai suta hata pan rasoda ma avaaj** avyo etle jova gaya to banne jana na modhe dahi na nishan ane dahi khali.
- Banne bhagya, LaxmiBaai **Ghanshyam pachad bhagya, pakdi ne baandhi didha**.
- Gaam ma bumo padta padta BhaktiMata na ghare gaya ke chor pakdyo chhe jova avo.
- Ghanshyam e **chamatkaar thi potani ni jagya e VeniRaam ne muki didho** ane pote ghare pochi gaya.
- Badha ne batava LaxmiBaai e rasodu kholyu to tya VeniRaam ane etla ma **ghare thi Ghanshyam gaud khata khata avya**.
- Badha hasi ne gharre gaya. LaxmiBaai ne thayu ke nakki aa baadak Bhagwan chhe. BhaktiMaataa na ghare jaine maafi mangi ane Ghanshyam ne pranam karya.


# 30 Ekaadashi no Mahima

- **Ekadashi** na divase ghanshyam **Ayodhya ma savare mandire darshan** karva gaya.
- **Mandir no mahant MohanDaas RaamCharit ni katha karto hato**. Ema vaat avi ke "**Ek ekadashi** kariye to **1000 AshwaMegh yagna jetlu punya male**."
- Aa saambhdi ne Ghanshyam e MohanDaas ne puchyu ke "Ghana loko ekadashi nathi karta, tenu karan shu?"
- MohanDaas - Bhagwan e aatlo durlabh deh aapyo ene kasht kem apvu. Jyarthi Jaggannath Puri ma Ekadashi ne uchi baandhi tyarthi loko ekadashi nathi karta. Jene khava naa madtu hoi ej ekadashi kare.
- Ghanshyam - Aavi khoti vaat na karso. Shastra virudh vaat karso to paap laagse, Ekadashi no bau mahima chhe etle ekadashi to karvij joiye.
- MohanDaas - Chhokra mane updesh ape chhe? Sharmaato nathi?
- Ghnashyam e ek drashti kari ema to bava ne samaadhi thai ane **jampuri ma yamduto ene marva lagya.**
- Bavaji potana aasanej bumo padta padta ucchadva lagyo. Hadka khokhraa thai gai pachi samadhi mathi baar ayo.
- Loko ni ane ghanshyam ni maafi ane kahyu ke aa to sarvopari bhagwan chhe ane hu ane tame badha pan Ekadashi karvani chalu kari do.



# 31 Ghanshyam ne Janoi aapi

- Ghanshyam **7 varas na thaya**. **Janoi** nu vichari ne BhaktiMaata e **gaam na gor HariKrushna Upaadhyay** ne bolavya.
- Pavitra ane saada bhraman, pothi kholi ne ganatri kari ane emne **Faagan Sud Dasham (Samvat 1845, Monday)** ni taarikh api.
- Janoi ni taiyaari chalu kari, aakhi barhatta sheri shangaari, ghar ma rang puravya, diwaal par chitro doravya, raangodi puraaavi, **leemda na jhaad niche moto mandap bandhyo**.
- HariKrushna Upaadhyay ane bija bhramano mantrochaar karva laagya.
- **RaamBali hajaam** ne bolai ne mundan karaavyu. Snaan kari pidu pitaambar pehryu. Devo avya ne pushpa varsha kari.
- **Bhramchaari no vesh dhaaran kari munj ane mekhlaa dharan karya, haath ma palaash dand ane bhiksha patra lidha**.
- Maata Pita ne page laagi, bhiksha maangi ne gor ne aapi.
- Pachi ghanshyam badvo dodva gaam na chauk ma avya.
- Ghanshyam ne thayu mare loko nu kalyan karvanu chhe, bhaagi ne himalay pochi jau ane tyathi bhaarat yaatra kari loko nu kalyaan karish.
- Ghanshyam bhaagya ane **maama VashraamBhaai** pachhad bhagyaa ane pakdi na  sakya.
- Temne mano mann Ghanshyam ne prarthana kari ke ghanshyam mari laaj rakho, hu Dharmdev, BhaktiMata ne su kahish.
- Ghanshyam e vicharyu ke Maata-Pita nirdosh chhe emne muki ne naa javai. Pacha aai gaya ane pakdaya.
- Mama emne pacha laya, hath ma 5 rupiya apya. Pachi maata sathe besi ne ghee, gaud, bhaat jamya.


# 32 RaamChandra rupe Darshan

- Ghanshyam badha mitro lai ne **Chhapaiya na Meen Sarovar** ma ramva gaya.
- Paani ma **pakad daaav ramva laagya**. Ghanshyam bau jhadap thi tare, koina hath ma ave nai.
- Thodi vaar pachi undi dubki maari, **sarovar na tad ma besi gaya**.
- Baadmitro ne chinta thai, emne thayu ke Ghanshyam kato dubi gaya kato magar laine khai gyo.
- Emne baar aaine tya **kinare Manchha (મંછા) naam na Dhobi** ne vaat kari.
- Dhobi e paani ma padi, dubkio mari ne bau tapaas kari pan koi madyu nai etle ene baadako kidhu ke ghanshyam na maata pita ne bolai lavo.
- Ek baadak bolai layo, **DharmDev, BhaktiMata, RaamPratap ane VashRaam** avya ane badhe vichaare ke have karvu shu?
- Ghanshyam ne thayu ke maata pita chinta ma chhe, have baar avu padse.
- Ghanshyam baar avya, paani par chali ne kinare avya, **paani par chalta hata** tyare badhane Ghanshyam ma **RaamChandra Bhagwan na darshan thaya**.
- Ghanshyam maata-pita ne page lagya ane kahyu ke chinta kem karo chho? Hu to niche besi ne maru kaam karto hato.
