# Prarambh Satsang Shikshan Pariksha — Syllabus & Notes

This repository contains study notes and the syllabus for the Prarambh Satsang Shikshan Pariksha. It is intended to help students prepare for the Prarambh-level satsang teaching exam by providing concise, well-organized material drawn from core satsang texts and teachings.

## What this repository contains
- Notes on **Ghanshyam Charita** — life, key events, selected passages, and essential lessons.
- Notes on **Kishore Satsang** — stories, teachings and moral lessons tailored for young satsangis (children and youth).
- Notes on **Yogiji Maharaj** — biography, major teachings, important speeches, and practical guidance.
- Selected excerpts and notes from related **Satsang Granths** that are part of the Prarambh syllabus.
- A clear, exam-focused syllabus overview linking topics to the notes for quick revision.

## Syllabus (Prarambh)
The repository organizes the syllabus into the following study areas. Each area includes summaries, important passages, and suggested questions for practice.

1. Ghanshyam Charita
   - Early life and formative events
   - Important incidents and their moral/teaching implications
   - Key verses and short explanations

2. Kishore Satsang
   - Core stories and parables for children
   - Moral teachings and how to present them to young satsangis
   - Sample lesson outlines and questions

3. Yogiji Maharaj
   - Life milestones and service history
   - Principal teachings and practical instructions for satsang life
   - Representative discourses and their meanings
